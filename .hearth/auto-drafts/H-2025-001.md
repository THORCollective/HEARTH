## Why
- Detecting the use of compromised credentials is critical as it can provide early warning of an attack. It can also help identify the source of the compromise and potentially prevent further exploitation.
- If this technique succeeds, the threat actors would gain access to the corporate network, potentially bypassing perimeter defenses and gaining a foothold from which they can launch further attacks.
- This specific implementation ties to larger campaigns as it is a common tactic used by ransomware groups to gain initial access to a target network. Once inside, they can deploy ransomware or other malicious payloads.
- The use of SonicWall VPN credentials was chosen over other techniques mentioned in the CTI due to the high actionability of this technique. VPN logs can provide clear evidence of unauthorized access attempts and can be monitored in real-time.
