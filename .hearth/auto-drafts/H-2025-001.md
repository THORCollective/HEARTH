## Why
- Detecting the use of compromised credentials is crucial as it is often the first step in a multi-stage attack. Identifying this behavior early can prevent further exploitation and lateral movement within the network.
- If this technique succeeds, adversaries gain a foothold in the network, which can lead to further exploitation, data exfiltration, or ransomware deployment.
- The use of compromised VPN credentials has been observed in multiple campaigns, indicating a broader trend among threat actors. This specific implementation ties to the Fog ransomware campaign.
- This technique was chosen over others mentioned in the CTI due to its high actionability (evident in logs), direct impact (enables adversary objectives), and uniqueness (distinctive of this specific threat).
