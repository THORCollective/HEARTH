## Why
- Detecting the use of stolen VPN credentials is crucial as it is often the first step in a targeted attack. This behavior allows adversaries to bypass perimeter defenses and gain a foothold within the network.
- If this technique succeeds, adversaries can gain unauthorized access to the network, which can lead to further exploitation and potentially significant damage.
- This specific implementation is tied to the Fog ransomware group's campaign, indicating a high level of sophistication and potential for significant impact.
- The use of stolen VPN credentials was chosen over other techniques mentioned in the CTI due to its direct impact on the adversary's ability to achieve their objectives and the clear evidence it leaves in logs/telemetry.
