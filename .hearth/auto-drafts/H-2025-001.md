## Why
- Detecting the use of compromised VPN credentials is critical as it is often the first step in an attack chain, allowing adversaries to gain a foothold within the network.
- If this technique succeeds, adversaries can potentially gain access to sensitive corporate resources, paving the way for further exploitation and lateral movement within the network.
- This specific implementation is tied to larger campaigns by the Fog ransomware group, who are known to use compromised VPN credentials for initial access.
- The use of compromised SonicWall VPN credentials was chosen over other techniques mentioned in the CTI due to its high actionability (evident in VPN logs), direct impact on adversary objectives (gaining network access), and its uniqueness to this specific threat (Fog ransomware group).
