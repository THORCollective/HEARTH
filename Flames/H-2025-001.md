# H-2025-001

Threat actors are leveraging Windows Management Instrumentation (WMI) to remotely execute malicious scripts on targeted systems, allowing them to gain initial access and establish footholds within the network.

## Why
- Detecting the use of WMI for remote execution of malicious scripts is critical as this technique can allow threat actors to gain initial access to targeted systems and establish a foothold within the network.
- Successful execution of this technique can lead to the escalation of privileges, lateral movement, and further compromise of the network.
- WMI is a legitimate tool used for system management tasks, which makes the malicious use of it difficult to detect and can potentially bypass security controls.
