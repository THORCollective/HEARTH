# H-2025-001

## Why
- Proxy tunnels allow attackers to hide the true source of their network connections, making it harder to detect and block malicious traffic
- Bypassing firewalls and other network controls enables lateral movement and further compromise of the victim environment 
- The CL-CRI-1014 threat actor cluster has been observed using Chisel tunnels as part of their playbook targeting financial institutions across Africa
