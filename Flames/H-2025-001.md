# H-2025-001

Threat actors are using PowerShell's Invoke-RestMethod cmdlet to download malicious payloads from Discord webhooks to evade network detection during initial access.

## Why
- Malicious packages are abusing Discord webhooks to exfiltrate data and download additional payloads
- Detecting abuse of trusted services like Discord for C2 is critical to identifying initial access 
- Catching malicious downloads early in the attack lifecycle can prevent later stages of compromise
