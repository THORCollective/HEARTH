# H-2025-001

Threat actors are using Classroom Spy, a remote administration tool marketed for schools, to monitor victim computers, collect files, log keystrokes, and execute commands on compromised hosts in the financial sector.

## Why
- Classroom Spy provides extensive remote access capabilities to attackers, allowing them to surveil users, steal sensitive data, and maintain control of infected systems. 
- By targeting the financial sector, attackers can gain access to valuable data and systems that can be exploited for financial gain or sold to other criminal groups.
- This activity is linked to a larger campaign (CL-CRI-1014) that has been targeting financial institutions across Africa since at least July 2023, likely acting as an initial access broker.
