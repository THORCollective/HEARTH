## Why
- Chisel tunnels enable threat actors to bypass firewalls and network monitoring to hide their malicious activities
- SOCKS proxies on infected hosts allow routing C2 traffic through the victim environment, making it harder to detect
- This tunneling tactic is a key part of the CL-CRI-1014 attack playbook targeting financial institutions across Africa
