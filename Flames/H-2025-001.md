# H-2025-001

Threat actors are embedding malicious URLs within SVG files for initial access, causing unsuspecting users to inadvertently download and execute the malicious payload upon opening the SVG file.

## Why
- Detecting this behavior is important as it allows for early identification of potential threats and can be used to prevent further infiltration of the network.
- If successful, this technique could lead to the threat actor gaining initial access to the network, potentially leading to further compromise and data loss.
- This technique has been linked to larger campaigns, indicating that it is a favored method among certain threat actors.
