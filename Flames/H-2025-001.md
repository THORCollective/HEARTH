# H-2025-001

## Why
- Chisel proxies allow attackers to hide their true origin by relaying connections through infected machines
- This technique enables lateral movement and data exfiltration while bypassing perimeter controls 
- The CL-CRI-1014 cluster has used this TTP to target financial institutions across Africa
