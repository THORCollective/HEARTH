# H-2025-001

Threat actors are leveraging WMI (Windows Management Instrumentation) to execute PowerShell scripts and deliver payloads, aiming to maintain persistence on compromised Windows servers.

## Why
- Detecting this behavior is crucial as it allows threat actors to maintain a stealthy presence on the compromised systems, making it harder for defenders to eradicate the threat.
- The successful execution of this technique indicates that the threat actors have already gained a significant level of access within the target system, and it can potentially lead to the exfiltration of sensitive data or further lateral movement within the network.
- The use of WMI for persistence is a common tactic observed across multiple threat campaigns, making it a high-priority detection target.
