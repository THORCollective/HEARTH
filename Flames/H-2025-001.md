# H-2025-001

Threat actors are using the open-source Chisel utility to create tunnels that proxy and forward network communications, enabling them to bypass firewall controls in compromised financial organizations.

## Why
- Chisel tunneling allows adversaries to hide their malicious traffic and evade network security controls. This can enable them to conduct further malicious activities undetected.
- Successfully establishing covert communication channels could allow the threat actors to maintain long-term access, move laterally to other systems, and exfiltrate sensitive financial data.
- This Chisel tunneling activity is part of a larger campaign by threat cluster CL-CRI-1014 that has been targeting financial institutions across Africa since at least July 2023, likely to establish initial access and then sell it to other threat actors on the dark web.
