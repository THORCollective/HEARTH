# H-2025-001

Threat actors are using the open-source Chisel utility to create tunnels that bypass network controls like firewalls and proxy C2 traffic to infected machines in the victim environment.

## Why
- Chisel tunneling allows the attackers to hide their C2 communications and evade network-based detection.
- By proxying through the Chisel tunnel, the attackers can reach additional systems, move laterally, and expand their access in the victim network.
- This Chisel tunneling is a key part of the CL-CRI-1014 attack playbook targeting financial institutions across Africa.
