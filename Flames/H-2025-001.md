# H-2025-001

Threat actors are using the Windows Management Instrumentation (WMI) system to execute malicious PowerShell scripts that establish persistence on the targeted Windows servers.

## Why
- Detecting this behavior is crucial as it indicates that the threat actors have gained a significant foothold in the network and are working to maintain their access.
- The tactical impact of a successful detection would be to disrupt the adversary's operations by removing their persistence mechanism, potentially forcing them to reveal additional TTPs in an attempt to regain access.
- This behavior has been linked to larger campaigns targeting critical infrastructure, making it a high priority for detection and mitigation.
