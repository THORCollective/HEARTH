# H-2025-001

Threat actors are injecting malicious JavaScript code obfuscated with the JSFireTruck technique into legitimate websites to redirect victims to malicious pages serving malware, exploits, and spam when referred from search engines.

## Why
- JSFireTruck obfuscation hides the malicious JavaScript's true purpose, hindering analysis and evading detection
- Compromised legitimate websites invisibly redirect victims to malicious pages that deliver malware, exploits, and spam
- The campaign's widespread scale, infecting thousands of websites, poses a significant threat to web users
