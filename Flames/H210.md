## Why
- Malicious advertising (malvertising) through Google Ads provides threat actors with a highly effective initial access vector by exploiting user trust in legitimate search results and brand impersonation
- This technique bypasses traditional email-based security controls and targets users actively searching for software downloads, increasing the likelihood of successful compromise
- The OXLOADER campaign demonstrates sophisticated evasion through low detection rates and multiple anti-analysis techniques, making early detection at the initial access stage critical before the payload executes
- Detecting malicious ad redirects can prevent the delivery of advanced loaders like OXLOADER that employ .reloc section abuse and MBA obfuscation to evade endpoint detection
